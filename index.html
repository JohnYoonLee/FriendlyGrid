How I Met Your Mother is a TV series when Ted, played by Josh Randor, is struggling to find a date after realizing his best friend Marshall, played by Jason Segal, proposes to his long time girlfriend Lily, played by Alyson Hannigan. With the help of his friends, including Barney, played by Neil Patrick Harris, he goes through-out New York with his wacky hijinks to try and find the love of his life.
